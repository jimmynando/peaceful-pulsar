It's 5 am, your alarm clock doesn't even ring yet but you have already waken up. You take a few minutes to get back to your body with flashes of the dream you had still popping in your head, but you can't remember in its totality.

You get up, stretch your back and go right to the kitchen to prepare some coffee. Since you quit smoking, coffee has become your ally.

While you wait the water to boil, you start to open the house. It's an old house your parents gave to you. Well, they don't really gave the house to you they just died and you stayed.

You notice something different, a silence that wasn't supposed to be there. You wake up early, but not that early. The bubbles brings you back to the current task.

Something isn't right. Your coffee is ready, you put it in a mug and go right to the balcony of the first floor, calmly but with anticipation.

While you take sips of your coffee you stare at an empty street.
