"Where am I?" I think as soon as I open my eyes. Only white walls around me, nothing more. I tried to scream but it seems I can't speak, it was always like that? I close my eyes and try to focus. My body starts to levitate, just a bit, like when I was learning to fly for the first time. I focus even more and I sense more people around me, not in my room, but in the facility. I regain my powers. I'll crush this place down.
