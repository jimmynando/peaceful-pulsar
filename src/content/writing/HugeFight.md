You're not the fighter type, never was. Everyone could tell this looking to your face or your body. You never work out or tried a martial art, that always sounded silly to you. "I'm a latino man, not build for protagonism" you would say.

That's your escape, the cynicism, it always was. You put yourself in that superiority position. Position that has, surpresingly, saved your neck from some dangerous situations. You know how to talk your way out of these kind of situations.

But deeply inside you, you know this day would come. Some day you would get the golden ticket. You know that some people resort in violence and somedy you would stumble in those people.

This day is today my friend. You were only paying for gas. Something you normally don't do cause you don't even have a car. But today you had to bring your father to the hospital.

It is your turn to pay, and suddenly someone break the line in front of you, like it was they turn. So what you gonna do chatty boy?

"Hey man, that's a line over here" - you say.

The man doesn't even respond to you, he just turns and punch you in the face. You were waiting for more pain, in fact. That wasn't so bad. You could take a few more of these, no problem. So you raise your arms and start to punch.
