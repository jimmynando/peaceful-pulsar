It's almost midnight, you have no clue were you at. You look around trying to find a recognizable place, but with no success. Just a green field, everywhere you look. Except this flag. It was already there? You don't remember seeing it at first, but it's there now and you're getting closer to it. No symbol or picture of any kind printed in this flag, just red.

A red flag could mean danger, nut in your current situation, means something, means curiosity, means a way to find something out.

You look down where the flag is craved and the floor here is different. Istead of grass you see dirt, like someone buried something. You take of the flag and with your bare hands you start digging.

Dirt is getting between your fingers and your nails, but you don't care. You need to know, what the flag signalizes. What is beyond this dirt and maybe an answer of who put it there.

You dig and you dig and you dig. But you don't need to dig anymore, your task is complete. You've found your prize, your answer, or maybe more questions.

A moving hand is right in front of you, or should I say, right above you, inside the hole you've dug. Nothing is attached to it, no body of some sort, nothing. Only a hand. A friendly hand, maybe?

That will sound crazy but it looks like it is looking at you, with curiosity. As soon as you approach it, it lay down opened, inviting you to a friendly handshake, you suppose.

So, you grab it and suddenly you see. The human whom the hand belongs to, and she smiles at you.
