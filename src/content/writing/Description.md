He wakes up almost screaming. Totally lost and confused. Where was he? He tries to squint his eyes but, without seeing anything, he decide to wait until they're get used to the darkness. Which mans it is dark outside. How much time has passed? What's the last thing he remembers?

He was at his house, it was fifteen past noon and then nothing, he was here.

For the looks of the place this is a warehouse. He's got a terrible headache, which probably means someone hit him. He tries to listen outside. The silence almost untouchable, except for the cicadas. It was summer.

In front of him, water and a plate with bread. He tries to reach it but realizes his arms are tied.
